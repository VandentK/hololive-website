Tokino Sora

Sora's character is depicted as a cheerful and energetic high school girl with long pink hair and blue eyes. She often refers to herself as the "hololive's number one idol" and is known for her catchy catchphrase "Nya~! Sora-chan here!"

Roboco san

Roboco is depicted as a humanoid robot with silver hair and red eyes. Her character design incorporates elements of both retro and futuristic aesthetics, giving her a unique and eye-catching appearance.

Yozora Mel

She is known for her sweet, calm and caring personality. She is a vampire prodigy from the Underworld but she is not into blood. She likes her acerola juice quite a lot.

Aki Rosenthal

She is a curious, fun-loving, friendly and determined high school half-elf girl from another universe with a very calm voice and attitude. She enjoys singing and belly dancing.

Akai Haato@Haachama

She is known by her chaotic alter-ego, Haachama (はあちゃま). Her name “Akai” translates to “red” and “Haato” is how Japanese pronounces the word “heart”, so she is strongly associated with the color red and hearts.

Shirakami Fubuki

She is part of a unit inside hololive called hololive Gamers (ホロライブゲーマーズ) alongside Ookami Mio, Nekomata Okayu and Inugami Korone. She is most known for her short-skit videos posted on Twitter and Youtube.

Natsuiro Matsuri

She is an energetic, expressive, and sometimes chaotic cheerleader girl who is bubbly as a festival.. Her cheerful attitude makes her popular among her peers and is looked up upon by her juniors.

Minato Aqua

She is a marine maid-styled virtual maid who is trying her very best, yet is still sometimes awkward and clumsy. She is a cute airhead maid who likes to prank her friends, but somehow it almost always backfires

Murasaki Shion

She is a self-proclaimed talented dark magician of noble birth who is actually an adult (or so she says). She is a smug, cheeky brat who speaks bluntly without the usual politeness/consideration associated with the Japanese language

Sakura Miko

Miko's character is a shrine maiden with a cheerful and outgoing personality. She is often depicted wearing a traditional shrine maiden outfit with pink hair and rabbit-like accessories.

Hoshimachi Suisei

Suisei's character is an idol from space who has come to Earth to entertain her viewers. She is often depicted with long, blue hair and a celestial-themed outfit.

Usada Pekora

Pekora's character is a mischievous and energetic rabbit who often displays a playful and somewhat chaotic personality. She is known for her distinctive laugh.

Yuzuki Choco

She is the school nurse at the Underworld Academy and is incredibly popular among the students. She loves sweets and often gets into trouble for leaving them strewn about on her desk.

Nakiri Ayame

She is a kimono-clad Oni girl from the Underworld Academy who loves to prank people. She is actually the president of the student council

Oozora Subaru

She is the manager of an MMA team and an e-sports club. She is a bright and cheerful girl full of spirit who can interact with anyone and everyone without hesitation.

Ookami Mio

She is described by her fans and other Hololivers as being mature, responsible and level-headed. She is also regarded as being exceptionally kind and many fans and peers frequently seek her personal advice.

Inugami Korone

She is a dog girl who works at a bakery and is known for her endurance streams where she streams the same game for very long periods of time, usually 5-12 hours.

Nekomata Okayu

Gawr Gura

She is a descendant of the Lost City of Atlantis who swam to Earth while saying “Its so boring down there LOLOLOL!” Gura bought her clothes (and her shark hat) in the human world and she really loves them.

Amelia Watson

She is a time-traveling detective who decided to become an idol herself after investigating hololive. She loves to pass her time training her reflexes with FPS games and challenging herself with puzzle games.

Kobo Kanaeru

She is a carefree girl who lives in the topmost room of a lighthouse and is actually the bloodline of the strongest rain shaman in the area. She has great potential to control rain, but she does not like to do it as a task.

Momosuzu Nene

She is a girl who came from another world in order to become an idol. She loves singing and dancing, and drawing. She trains hard every day to become someone loved by many

Ceres Fauna

She is a member of the Council and the Keeper of “Nature,” the second concept created by the Gods. She has materialized in the mortal realm as a druid in a bid to save nature.

Vestia Zeta

She is the mysterious 7th recruit of The Secret Archive Unit that has a full license to collect virtual data.
